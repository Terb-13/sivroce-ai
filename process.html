A plan you can follow

Three steps from assessment to ownership.

No mystery phases. No vendor lock-in. Just a clear path from where you are today to a team that runs AI independently.

Step 1 — Assess & Align

Map the real workflow. Align on the highest-ROI starting point.

We embed with your team to understand how work actually flows — not how the process manual says it should. Together we identify the workflow where AI delivers measurable impact fastest, and define success metrics your leadership team agrees on.

  • On-site or virtual workflow mapping sessions
  • Data readiness assessment across ERP, files, and tribal knowledge
  • Prioritized roadmap with timeline, investment, and expected ROI

Typical deliverables

Workflow opportunity brief
Aligned success metrics & KPIs
90-day implementation plan

Duration: 1–2 weeks

Step 2 — Build & Deploy

Build the system. Integrate with what you have. Ship to production.

We develop AI workflows tailored to your environment — connecting to your ERP, email, file shares, and shop floor systems where it matters. You see working software early and often, with your team's feedback shaping every iteration.

  • Custom AI agents, automations, and decision-support tools
  • Integration with existing systems — no rip-and-replace required
  • Pilot with real data, then phased production rollout

Typical deliverables

Production AI workflow(s)
System integrations & APIs
Performance monitoring dashboard

Duration: 4–6 weeks

Step 3 — Train & Handoff

Train your team. Transfer everything. You own it completely.

This is where most AI projects fail — and where we refuse to cut corners. We run hands-on training sessions, build internal champions, document every system, and transfer all code and assets. When we leave, your team runs it without us.

  • Role-based training for operators, supervisors, and IT
  • Complete documentation, runbooks, and troubleshooting guides
  • Full source code, prompts, and infrastructure-as-code transfer

Typical deliverables

Operations playbook & training materials
Repository access & deployment guides
Certified internal champions

Duration: 1–2 weeks
