Our Approach

Three steps from assessment to ownership.

Handoff and ownership are designed in from the start. We embed as builders inside your operation — not observers writing reports from the sidelines.

Philosophy: embedded builders, value-gated delivery

We don't bill for open-ended discovery. Each phase has defined deliverables and a clear gate before moving forward. You see working software early. Your team's feedback shapes every iteration. And we structure every engagement so your team gains the capability to own, maintain, and extend the work at any point.

Built so your team can take over seamlessly — we leave when you are ready.

Map the real workflow. Align on highest-ROI starting point.

We embed with your team to understand how work actually flows. Together we identify where AI delivers measurable impact fastest and define success metrics your leadership agrees on.

  • Workflow mapping sessions on-site or virtual
  • Data readiness assessment across ERP, files, tribal knowledge
  • Prioritized roadmap with timeline and expected ROI

Deliverables

  • Workflow opportunity brief
  • Aligned success metrics
  • Implementation plan with value gates

Typical duration: 1–2 weeks

Build the system. Integrate with what you have. Ship to production.

Custom AI workflows connected to your ERP, email, file shares, and shop floor systems. Working software early and often — no rip-and-replace required.

  • AI agents, automations, and decision-support tools
  • Integration with existing systems
  • Pilot with real data, phased production rollout

Deliverables

  • Production AI workflow(s)
  • System integrations & APIs
  • Performance monitoring dashboard

Typical duration: 4–6 weeks

Train your team. Transfer everything. You own it completely.

Full source code, prompts, documentation, and runbooks are transferred to you. We run hands-on training, build internal champions, and offer a defined transition support window if you want a safety net.

  • Role-based training for operators, supervisors, and IT
  • Complete documentation and troubleshooting guides
  • Repository access and deployment guides in your accounts

Deliverables

  • Operations playbook & training materials
  • Full source & infrastructure transfer
  • Certified internal champions

Typical duration: 1–2 weeks

Every engagement ends with independence.

The goal is never retention — it's capability. Built so your team can take over seamlessly — we leave when you are ready. Your team gains the capability to own, maintain, and extend the work at any point. Every project also creates clean data foundations for future robotics, vision, and physical automation.
