About Sirvoce

Manufacturing operators who build AI — not consultants who advise about it.

We're a boutique, practitioner-led firm built exclusively for mid-sized manufacturers ($10M–$500M). We combine real plant-floor experience with modern AI engineering to deliver working systems, train your team, and hand off everything.

Operating principles

Builders, not advisors

We write code, deploy systems, and sit with your team on the floor. Slide decks are not deliverables.

Ownership by design

Handoff and ownership are designed in from the start. Full source code, prompts, documentation, and runbooks are transferred to you.

Zero lock-in

No recurring license fees for systems we build. No proprietary platforms you can't leave. You own the stack.

Transparency always

Audit trails, inspectable models, and human-in-the-loop approval. Your team controls what AI recommends.

Speed with substance

Working prototypes in weeks, not years of discovery. Value-gated phases keep momentum and accountability.

Built for what's next

Every project creates clean data foundations for robotics, vision systems, and physical automation.
