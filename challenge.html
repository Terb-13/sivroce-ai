The Challenge

Legacy workflows are quietly eroding your margins.

Mid-sized manufacturers ($10M–$500M) carry a unique burden: complex enough operations to need custom solutions, but without the enterprise budget or IT bench to absorb another failed transformation project.

Rising cost-to-serve

SKU proliferation, custom orders, and exception-heavy workflows drive up the cost of every transaction while customers expect faster turnaround at the same price.

Manual core processes

Quoting, estimating, scheduling, and proofing run on spreadsheets, email chains, and tribal knowledge. Cycle times stretch while errors compound.

Legacy systems & digital friction

Your ERP and MES weren't designed for intelligent automation. Data lives in silos and integrations are fragile.

Talent stretched thin

Your best people spend days on repetitive coordination instead of high-value problem solving. Burnout rises as automation pressure builds.

AI pilots that never ship

Big consultancies deliver slide decks. Internal teams lack bandwidth. Proof-of-concepts sit in sandboxes while the shop floor keeps running the old way.

Automation readiness gap

Robotics and advanced automation are on the horizon — but without clean data, documented workflows, and internal AI capability, you won't be ready.

You don't need another strategy document.

You need a practitioner who has been on the plant floor, understands your constraints, and can deliver a working system your team will own permanently.
